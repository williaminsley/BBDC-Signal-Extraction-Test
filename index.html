Browser Signal Extraction Lab

This lab is for pre-release behavioural signal extraction. The aim is to expose as many browser/device signals as possible so we can judge which ones are genuinely available and usable for CA Prototype 2.

How to use this page

Start a session, grant optional permissions, then work through every zone naturally for 10–20 seconds each. Try taps, swipes, holds, multi-touch, drag, scroll, typing, edits, selection, paste/copy/cut if possible, orientation changes, and app switching. Export the log at the end.

Tap / pointer zone
Tap repeatedly, press and hold, short moves, repeated presses, and leave/re-enter the zone.
Swipe zone
Perform short and long swipes in multiple directions. On desktop, also move the mouse through the zone.
Pinch / multi-touch zone
Use two fingers if available. Try pinch in/out, rotate slightly, and lift one finger before the other.
Wheel zone
On laptop/desktop, use wheel/trackpad scrolling and small/large gestures. On phone, normal touch events will still be captured.
Scroll zone
Scroll naturally up and down several times, including overscroll/bounce if the browser allows it.
